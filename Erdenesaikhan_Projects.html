CSP

This project was the first of many projects in my Csp 1 class. I developed the code for this project alongside my partner who focused on the documentation side of the project. Our idea for this project was that the player could freely move their character, but the character would have to reach a certain goal to win. Our theme of space was also unanimous in the creation of the project and after a day of planning we started to make the game a reality.

Instructions
